# Answer: One thing QX does

**QX provides a DOT framework** — a simple intent scaffold that makes your goals explicit and actionable.

## What DOT does:

- **⟡ Declaration** — what you will do
- **◎ Outcome** — what "done" looks like  
- **⇌ Transaction** — the steps to get there

Think of DOT as a **recipe card**: your intent → finished state → steps.

This structure helps GPTs understand your goals clearly and provide more reliable, aligned assistance.
